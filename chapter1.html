Chapter 1: Monkey and banana

It was Halloween night, the spookiest day of the year. The wind blew restlessly and dauntingly in a quiet neighborhood. All houses were decorated with pumpkins and scary stuff and some families had even put up some festive lights on their front lawns.

Three friends, Olivia, Sarah and Tim had gathered at Olivia's house. The trio had been friends since and it was their yearly tradition to spend Halloween night together at one their houses. They'd watch movies and listened some music. Though the main event of the evening, one that everyone was impatiently waiting for, was of course The Costumes .

Olivia loved the candy and would probably eat of their loot before they finished their round. Sarah and Tim however, were more excited about their costumes. Especially Sarah couldn't wait to show off their costume that their had for them. So as soon as the movie ended Sarah brought up the costumes...


Sarah: "Shouldn't we get into our costumes? Let's rock the party!
Olivia: "No way, it's way too early. Aren't we heading out after 11 pm? Besides, my stupid costume is too small."
Tim: "Actually, I should go by my house really quickly and get Fifi"
Olivia: "Wait what, are you bringing your banana trick-or-treating?"
Tim: "Yep! Fifi is part of my costume! I'll be a monkey and Fifi will be my snack."

Olivia and Sarah sighed. Both of them doubted whether it would be a good idea to to dress as a monkey and take Fifi with them.

Tim: "Oh come one! It will be awesome! Just give me a minute and I'll go get Fifi and we can head out then."

Tim looked at their friends pleadingly. The other two considered the idea. Tim was clearly excited about their idea and the other two did not want to spoil his fun. And after all, what could even go wrong?

Olivia: "Okay, fine. Go get Fifi. We'll listen some music while we wait for you."

"Yes, awesome! Oh we're gonna look so cool! This is going to be the best Halloween yet", Tim exclaimed with excitment, jumped off the couch and ran out of the living room. Soon the other two heard something.
