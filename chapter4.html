In the house

The house smelled like death and murder as the kids walk across the big hall. The house seems to be empty but still they can't help feeling like weird.

"Look! That door is open!" She points to a door quickly.

"Could Tim's pet could have gone there? Maybe they already returned outside...", quite whispers their voice trembling.

"No they definitely are here", says Sarah who has reached the open door first. "I can see their footsteps in the dust. And they are only going one way...", scared gulps.

The others gather next to them and look down narrow stairway that must go in to the basement of the house. However, all they can see in the end of the stairs is complete darkness...

But then they hear something from outside. They are zombies!

What now?
