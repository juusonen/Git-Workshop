18 hours later...

When Tim got back with their pet banana, Olivia and Sarah had already danced on the ceiling. While Sarah was standing in front of the mirror adjusting their socks, Olivia was sitting on the hallway floor wondering whether the rain would ever stop or whether they should ask their mum for an extra pair of wellies for Fifi the banana.

"Oh finally!" Olivia sighed when Tim, dressed as Elvis, opened the front door. "What took you so long?" Sarah asked.

"The coding workshop took longer than I thought", Tim moaned in frustration holding Fifi dressed as a cowgirl. "How was the disco? Did you have a good time while I was gone?" Tim asked.

"No, Sarah can't do the lifts like you can. But hurry up, we need to head out now! Otherwise we'll get apples or something when people run out of candy", Olivia snapped, stood up and straightened their Jesus costume. "Come on, let's go!"


Forty-five minutes later...

"That was lame. Our neighbors were already almost out of candy!", Olivia huffs and shoots an angry look at Tim.

"We could go to Elm Street", Tim proposes. "They are quite a few houses there and most kids don't go there."

"Yeah because Old Man McGee lives there!", Sarah states with a worried look on their face. They won't admit it to their friends but they are actually a little bit afraid of Old Man McGee.

"What's the big deal? You're not afraid of a little old man, are you?", Olivia smirks.

"Of course I'm not!" Sarah scoffs but looks still nervous.

"Well let's just go then! Let's get that candy!" Olivia says sounding cocky. They start leading the group towards Elm Street. Tim hesitates for a moment and then sprints after the others.
